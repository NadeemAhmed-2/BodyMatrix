Installing BodyMatrix

Follow these steps to install the APK on your Android device. This takes about 2 minutes.

  1. 1
    Download the APK using the button below. The file will save to your Downloads folder.
  2. 2
    On your Android phone, go to Settings → Security → Install Unknown Apps and enable it for your browser or Files app.
  3. 3
    Open your Downloads folder, tap the BodyMatrix.apk file, and tap Install when prompted.
  4. 4
    Once installed, open BodyMatrix, create your account, and start building your ideal body. 🔥
